Ian Blancaflor (born November 17, 1998), on the Internet referred to as Ian Bla, is a singer and songwriter from Kapolei, Hawaii.

After a whole summer of playing Breath of the Wild and lying constantly to his now-tired audience, he released his debut EP, Out of State, in 2018. Then, after a whole semester of playing Super Smash Bros. and lying constantly to his definitely-tired audience, he released his debut album, Edit Profile

He goes to college in New Jersey but acts like he lives in New York City, living his life as a role model for all disillusioned STEM majors.

With his silly and archaic Lenovo ThinkPads, and a cheap condenser microphone from the bald head of real life Jeff Bezos, he sings and writes and plays guitar for all the foolish people out there.
